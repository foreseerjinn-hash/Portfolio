Laughing and playing on the internet while the dark tower in the distance grows a little taller each day...


Hi, I’m J, a self-taught web developer at the beginning of an extensive journey into the soft dev netherverse.


...I started learning to code this year, with the initial intention of exploring more analogue web design.

I was motivated by the days of old when everyone and their dog had a website, and knew the basics of coding.
I began asking myself, “What if we could just do this again?”

Six months later, I deployed my first site .

Marking the beginning of a hands-on approach to building for the web. During this time, I’ve been developing multiple sites while expanding my understanding of modern web technologies and workflows.

My interests sit at the intersection of design, accessibility, privacy, and social equity. I’m especially curious about how software, specifically web platforms and machine learning, can be used by and for marginalised communities, in the hopes of building healthier online spaces.

Currently, I’m exploring microblog-style platforms as an alternative to mainstream social media, with a focus on accessibility, encryption, and user privacy. I’m actively learning JavaScript and working through the challenges of designing and consuming APIs for secure data storage and retrieval.
While my ambitions are large, my approach is practical: build, learn, deploy, repeat. Each project helps me better understand not only how the internet works, but how it should work.
